## [From vision to reality: Microsoft’s partners embrace AI to deliver customer value](https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2024/03/20/from-vision-to-reality-microsofts-partners-embrace-ai-to-deliver-customer-value/)

Date: Mar 20, 2024

### Summary

Microsoft is expanding its AI Cloud Partner Program to help partners create AI-powered solutions. The program includes solutions partner designations, specializations, and technologies. With new features like the AI Practice Development Playbook and AI Development Acceleration Toolkit, Microsoft aims to help partners stand out in the market. The number of partners developing solutions with Microsoft Azure AI has grown significantly, indicating a year of innovation and growth. This expansion will provide partners with the resources they need to develop their AI capabilities and better serve their customers.
